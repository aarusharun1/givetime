How It Works

No more digging through search results or misleading descriptions. GiveTime organizes opportunities so you can find what actually fits you.

Our Story

Who We Are

GiveTime started with a simple problem: finding the right volunteer opportunity shouldn't be this hard. Search results were misleading, organizations were often difficult to find, and there was no single place to see what was actually available in the community.

So, a student at Novi High School built the solution. GiveTime is a volunteer database that cuts through the noise and gives you honest, organized information about where and how you can help. It gives users a quick and easy way to find the perfect organization that fits their wants and needs.

What started as a local project now serves the Southeast Michigan area and continues to grow. GiveTime is currently expanding into additional regions and plans to feature hundreds of new organizations across different causes and communities.

Congressional App Challenge Winner

GiveTime was selected as the winning app for Michigan's 6th Congressional District in the 2024 Congressional App Challenge, hosted by Rep. Debbie Dingell.
